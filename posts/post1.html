What I Learned from CSS Layout Practice

By Noor Fatima • 05 April 2026 • 7 min read

In this post I share beginner lessons from CSS practice. I focused on layout, spacing, colors, and making pages look neat without using any advanced frameworks.

Using Flexbox for easy alignment

Flexbox made it easy to align cards and navigation links. I used it for row and column layouts instead of trying complex positioning.

layout.css (flexbox) 
.card-row {
  display: flex;
  gap: 16px;
  flex-wrap: wrap;
}

What I Learned

I now understand how to make my website look clean and organized on both mobile and desktop. This practice helped me a lot in making my blog look beautiful.
